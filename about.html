INITIAL REPORT

Ryan William Duncuft (born September 15, 2007) is a rapidly emerging British rapper, singer, and songwriter from the Greater Manchester town of Mossley. Known professionally as Ryze Tha Kidd, his music is defined by a distinctive fusion of hip hop, melodic emo rap, and hard-hitting trap music. Operating as a fiercely independent artist, he is not only the voice of his music but also the architect, self-producing a significant portion of his discography using FL Studio and delivering his work through his own label, Ryze Collective Media Group.

ORIGIN & GENESIS

Duncuft embarked on his musical journey in 2019, drawing inspiration from a diverse palette of genres, including rock, alternative, and, crucially, the emotive depth of the late Juice WRLD. His early work was a testament to his raw talent, initially recorded using little more than a cell phone and basic earphones on the BandLab app. This period of resourcefulness laid the foundation for his hands-on approach, quickly developing into proficiency in music production.

ARTISTIC TRAJECTORY

Ryze Tha Kidd quickly made a name for himself in the UK music scene with his innovative, genre-blending sound. His music often serves as a candid reflection of his personal experiences, resonating with a growing audience who connect with his raw authenticity and melodic complexity.

EXTENDED VENTURES

Demonstrating a vision that extends far beyond music, Ryze Tha Kidd actively expands his creative and business portfolio. He is the founder of Ryze VFX Film, a production company dedicated to visual storytelling. Furthermore, he is developing RTK+, a comprehensive streaming platform set to become the exclusive home for his official content.
