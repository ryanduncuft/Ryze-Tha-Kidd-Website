INITIAL REPORT

**Ryan William Duncuft** (born September 15, 2007) is a rapidly emerging **British rapper, singer, and songwriter** from the Greater Manchester town of Mossley. Known professionally as **Ryze Tha Kidd**, his music is defined by a distinctive fusion of **hip hop, melodic emo rap, and hard-hitting trap music**. Operating as a fiercely independent artist, he is not only the voice of his music but also the architect, **self-producing** a significant portion of his discography using **FL Studio** and delivering his work through his own label, **Ryze Collective Media Group**.

ORIGIN & GENESIS

Duncuft embarked on his musical journey in **2019**, drawing inspiration from a diverse palette of genres, including rock, alternative, and, crucially, the emotive depth of the late **Juice WRLD**. His early work was a testament to his raw talent, initially recorded using little more than a cell phone and basic earphones on the BandLab app. This period of resourcefulness laid the foundation for his hands-on approach, quickly developing into proficiency in music production, a process that allowed him to solidify his unique sonic identity as he entered his teens.

ARTISTIC TRAJECTORY

Ryze Tha Kidd quickly made a name for himself in the UK music scene with his innovative, genre-blending sound. His music often serves as a candid reflection of his personal experiences, resonating with a growing audience who connect with his raw authenticity and melodic complexity. As a self-contained artist and producer, he continues to push the boundaries of his sound, building a body of work that is entirely his own.

EXTENDED VENTURES

Demonstrating a vision that extends far beyond music, Ryze Tha Kidd actively expands his creative and business portfolio. He is the founder of **Ryze VFX Film**, a production company dedicated to visual storytelling and high-concept music videos. Furthermore, he is developing **RTK+**, a comprehensive streaming platform set to become the exclusive home for his official music videos, behind-the-scenes content, and future film projects, showcasing his multifaceted artistic and entrepreneurial ambitions.
